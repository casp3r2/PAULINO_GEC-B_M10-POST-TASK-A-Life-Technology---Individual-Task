JM'S BLOG JOURNEY

Throughout his day to day life JM PAULINO has encountered many situations that required technological experience to make his day mor easier

Being present with just a cellphone was enough for me to have as much information as much as possible. In light of this was during our family trip to japan, we would already have the itinerary. But in every destination there should always be a prepared google maps or any navigation system for me and my family to know when we are close to the location, example would be from going to a known park in japan is Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden which was an easy 10-20 min walk that saved us money from using taxi/cab as it is very expensive to use and while just walking to the park using resourceful navigation would also show the type of trains and bus to use for a more cheaper mode of transportation. Below would show photos of the locations we have been through that is mostly restaurants and popular locations that were taken by me and some my family.

Collecting informational needs in modes of transportation and navigation.

A major complex problem for us travelling out of the country is to understand the menu as some wont offer english versions. So as a tech savvy person, we always have to carry a portable wifi so that we can always have our google translator ready as it has an option to translate any text to english by just opening my camera through the app. In this day and age we always have to be that prepared and know the latest technological advancements we have today and this translator apps are a key cornerston to help for day to day livelyhood for us that dont understand other languages. Below would show pictures taken by me and by my family of restaurants that we have eaten that went well due to having a prepared translator.
